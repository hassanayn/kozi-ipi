# Data License and Source Notes

Kozi Ipi includes processed education data compiled from public and official sources, including NACTVET, TCU, VETA, Ministry of Education notices, and Zanzibar vocational sources.

We do not claim ownership over official government or institution source materials. The processed dataset is provided for education discovery, research, and public-interest use.

If you use this data, verify important decisions against the original official source before acting on it. Programme availability, fees, entry requirements, accreditation status, and intake details can change.

Contributions that improve accuracy, add official source links, correct outdated information, clarify eligibility requirements, or identify records needing review are welcome.

## Data Contribution Rules

- Prefer official regulator or institution sources.
- Do not guess missing values.
- Keep uncertain values marked as `unknown` or `needsReview`.
- Include a source URL for every correction when possible.
- Do not add private student data or scraped exam-result data.
